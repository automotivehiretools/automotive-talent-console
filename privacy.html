Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Automotive Hire Tools respects your privacy. This website is designed to operate without collecting personal data.

1. No Data Collection

This website does not:

  • Ask you to create an account
  • Provide login or registration forms
  • Store user profiles or usage history on a server
  • Use cookies, tracking pixels or analytics scripts
  • Log IP addresses or device information for profiling

2. Local Browser Storage

Some tools may use your browser’s local storage to remember recent searches on your device only. This data:

  • Stays on your own device
  • Is not sent to any server controlled by Automotive Hire Tools
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data

3. Third-Party Websites

Our tools generate Google search links and may help you find third-party websites (e.g., job boards, social networks, company sites). Your interactions with those websites are governed by their own Privacy Policies and Terms.

4. Contact

If you contact us directly by email (for example, for feedback or support), any personal data you share in that email is used only to respond to your message and is not added to any marketing list.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page, and your continued use of the site after changes are published indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

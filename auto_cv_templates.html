2. Garage / Bodyshop Cover Letter & Messages

Short email / cover letter

Subject: Vehicle Technician – CV attached

Dear [Name] / Hiring Manager,

I am a [Level 3-qualified] Vehicle Technician with experience in [servicing / diagnostics /
brakes / suspension etc.]. I am very interested in any current or upcoming opportunities
you may have at [garage / bodyshop name].

I am confident working in a busy workshop, following job cards and delivering safe, high
quality work for customers. My CV is attached with more detail on my skills and experience.

If you are open to a short conversation, I would be happy to discuss how I can support
your team.

Kind regards,
[Your name]
[Phone number]

WhatsApp message to garages

Hi, my name is [Name]. I'm a [qualified / apprentice] vehicle technician based in [area].
I'm looking for work and wondered if you are hiring, or may need extra help.

I have experience with [servicing / diagnostics / bodywork – adjust to you]. Would it be ok
to send my CV over for you to keep on file?

Thank you.
